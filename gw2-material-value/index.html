What is this?
This tool gets information on the materials in your bank account and computes estimates of the combined values of those materials if you sold them now on the trading post.

Is my API key safe?
Yes, your data is never actually sent to my server. All the computations happen in your browser which directly communicates with the ArenaNET server. If you don't believe me, ask the IT technician of your choice to have a look at this page. ;)

How accurate is the computed amount?
That's hard to say. The tool gets the highest buy orders and lowest sell orders to determine the value of your materials. Those might be inaccurate if you have a lot more materials than are being sold/bought at those prices. So the actual value might deviate in both directions - this might actually make it kind of accurate, in the end, though.
